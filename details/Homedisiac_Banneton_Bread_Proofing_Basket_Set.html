Homedisiac Banneton Bread Proofing Basket Set

The Homedisiac banneton bread proofing basket set is an essential tool for any baker, whether you're just starting out or a seasoned artisan. Fabricated entirely from 100% natural rattan, these baskets are built to last, offering durability and water resistance without the use of harmful chemicals.

Their smooth, non-stick surface ensures that your dough won't adhere to the basket, eliminating the need for greasy or chemical-laden treatments. Paired with an included linen liner and dough scraper, cleaning becomes a breeze, saving you time and effort in the kitchen.

This set features a 9-inch round and a 10-inch oval basket, allowing you to shape a variety of bread loaves at once. It's designed to hold up to 1.5 pounds of dough, making it perfect for working with sourdough and other artisan bread varieties. Whether you're baking for your family or a professional kitchen, the Homedisiac banneton set enhances the baking process with its elegant design and functional benefits, delivering excellent results every time.
